Usage Tips

Basic Usage

Step 1: Tap the extension icon on the left side of the address bar

Tap the icon on the left side of the address bar

Step 2: Tap "Danmaku Flow Chat" icon from the extension list

Tap "Danmaku Flow Chat" to open settings screen

Step 3: Adjust settings and tap "Close"

After changing settings to your preference, tap "Close"

Done: Danmaku display setup is now complete!

  • You can change font size and display duration to your preference
  • Danmaku is more visible when viewing the stream in landscape mode
  • Settings can be changed anytime using the same steps
How to view comments in archives?

Step 1: Tap the extension icon on the left side of the address bar

Tap the icon on the left side of the address bar

Step 2: Check "Danmaku Flow Chat" from the extension list

"Danmaku Flow Chat" will be displayed in the extension list

Step 3: Select "Request Desktop Website" from the "..." menu

Turn on "Request Desktop Website" from the menu

Done: Desktop version of Twitch will show archive chat history

How to view on the largest screen possible?

Step 1: Tap the extension icon on the left side of the address bar

Tap the icon on the left side of the address bar

Step 2: Check "Danmaku Flow Chat" from the extension list

"Danmaku Flow Chat" will be displayed in the extension list

Step 3: Select "Hide Toolbar" from the "..." menu

Select "Hide Toolbar" from the menu
